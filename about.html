About This Site

The Delusional Cowboys Fan is a satirical, fan-powered look at the emotional rollercoaster of supporting the Dallas Cowboys. We chronicle the five weekly stages of Cowboy fandom — from tortured hope to full-blown delusion — using real tweets and moments from across the fanbase.

This site is not affiliated with the Dallas Cowboys or the NFL. All content is used for entertainment purposes only.
